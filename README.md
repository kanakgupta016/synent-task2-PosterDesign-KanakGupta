1. Objective :

To create a bold and attractive promotional poster for Amazon’s “Great Indian Sale” campaign that quickly grabs attention and highlights discounts, offers, and shopping categories.

2. Steps Performed :
    
  a. Planned the poster layout and hierarchy
  b. Used bold typography and high-contrast colours
  c. Added discount tags, sale elements, and product visuals
  d. Included offer details, dates, and branding
  e. Balanced the composition for a clean and impactful look


3. Tools Used : 

  a. Adobe Photoshop
  b. Adobe Illustrator

4. Outcome :
  
The final design delivers a modern and energetic e-commerce advertisement that effectively promotes sale offers and creates excitement for online shoppers.
